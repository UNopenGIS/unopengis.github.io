DWG 6: OpenDroneMap and 3D

'OpenDroneMap and 3D' working group is the 6th domain working group (DWG) of the UN Open GIS Initiative. It aims to search for open source software used to build 3D data from video or imagery data captured by mobile or aerial devices such as drones and mobile phones, and tests the software in the field.

Practice: MINUSCA '3D Bria camp' with OpenDroneMap(led by OpenDroneMap Community)
